Priorities

The target uncertainty has been defined as the 1/12 of the target\'s transit duration (in 2029).
  • CAMPAIGN: Planet included in one of the curent observing campaings.
  • ALERT: An O-C greater than 10 minutes observed by ExoClock participants.
  • HIGH: Prediction uncertainty greater than the target.
  • MEDIUM: Last observation older than 1 year, or observing coverage lower that 50%.
  • LOW: Else, or observed at 3 different epochs in the last 6 months.
